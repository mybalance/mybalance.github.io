What We're About

We exist to help and support you in reducing the amount of harmful greenhouse gasses produced by your day-to-day lifestyle.

Balance has been designed to help individuals make decisions and take control of the things in your life that contribute to climate change.

The App

Our easy to use app helps you to quickly establish a baseline historical carbon footprint, from which a carbon reduction target can be established.

Balance can analyse your historical footprint data to find 'easy wins', helping you shrink your footprint quickly and easily.

Offsetting

Balance also offers bespoke carbon offsetting packages, which can help you reach a net carbon neutral footprint.

If you choose to offset, you gain access to a rich set of tools which can collect data from your daily life to compare with your targets.

The World Around Us

The world around us is ever changing, at Balance we'll do our best to keep you updated on the top stories relating to Our Planet and climate change.

We'll post regular updates to the news feed, with commentary on current events, and tips to help you live a lower carbon lifestyle.

If you, like us, care about Our Planet and the impact our actions are having, then we look forward to you joining us on this journey!
