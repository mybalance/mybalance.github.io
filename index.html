The Problem

The warming of our planet, highlighted by the record breaking high temperatures, melting polar caps, and rising sea level, are all visible signs our planet is under severe pressure.

Most research points towards the levels of carbon dioxide (and other harmful greenhouse gasses) as the main cause. The levels of carbon dioxide in our atmosphere are the highest ever recorded - we are quite literally suffocating our planet.

Our Solution

It's not too late to make the changes required to avoid a full scale failure, but your action is required now. Balance has been designed so that we as individuals can easily understand the impact of our actions and help change the direction that our planet is heading in.

Balance operates on your smartphone to dynamically capture and manage the carbon you generate in daily life, with easy to use help menu and handy tips you can join the millions of people who are doing something about their carbon footprint.
