Our Situation

At a time where Carbon Dioxide levels have reached record highs, many climate change experts believe that the lifestyle we follow has placed us at the point of crisis.

In the UK each person generates an average of 4 times the sustainable limit for planet Earth.

If you care about our planet, and believe it should be here for future generations, you likely need to make some changes.

Balance has been designed to operate on your smartphone, helping you join the millions of people who are doing something positive to live a lower carbon lifestyle.

Take the 4 Steps

Calculate your carbon footprint, and compare to the UK average. This should take around 5 minutes, it's free to use and will form the basis of your next steps.

Using the lifestyle advice within Balance, start making changes to reduce your carbon footpriont. Some are really quick and easy, others will take more commitment.

Build up an accurate assessment of your day-to-day carbon output using Balance. Each time you drive, fly, use public transport, or use energy in your home, data can be recorded. This can be tracked against your target, to see the impact of the changes you've made.

Balance gives you the option to offset the carbon you can't remove through lifestyle changes alone. Using projects that are credible & certified to reduce greenhouse gasses, your direct action will will achieve a carbon neutral footprint. You can be confident that you're joining millions of other people who are tackling climate change for our future generations.
